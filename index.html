Foods with High Protein

If you're looking to increase your protein intake, here are some foods to consider:

  1. Chicken breast
  2. Salmon
  3. Eggs
  4. Beans
  5. Chickpeas
  6. Lentils
  7. Quinoa
  8. Greek yogurt

Benefits of High Protein Foods

  • Helps build and repair tissues in the body
  • Helps boost metabolism
  • Increases feelings of fullness and satiety
  • Helps maintain muscle mass

Protein-Rich Recipe

Here's a delicious and easy recipe that's packed with protein:

  • Grilled chicken breast with a side of quinoa and roasted veggies

Protein Supplements

If you're having trouble meeting your daily protein needs through food, you may want to consider taking a protein supplement. Some options include:

  • Whey protein powder
  • Casein protein powder
  • Soy protein powder
  • Pea protein powder

Protein Intake Recommendations

According to the National Academies of Sciences, Engineering, and Medicine, the recommended daily protein intake for adults is:

Daily Protein Intake Recommendations
Age Group Recommended Daily Protein Intake
Adults 0.8 grams per kilogram of body weight
Pregnant and lactating women 1.1 grams per kilogram of body weight
Athletes and active individuals 1.2-2.0 grams per kilogram of body weight

Conclusion

Getting enough protein in your diet is important for overall health and wellness. Be sure to include some of these high protein foods in your meals and snacks throughout the day.
